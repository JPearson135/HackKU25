MindfulMate Disclaimer

Important Notice

MindfulMate is an AI assistant designed to provide emotional support and general guidance. It is NOT a substitute for professional mental health services, therapy, or medical advice.

Limitations of Service

  • MindfulMate is not a licensed therapist, counselor, or healthcare provider.
  • The advice provided is general in nature and not tailored to specific medical or psychological conditions.
  • MindfulMate cannot diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or provide crisis intervention.
  • The AI has limited knowledge and may not always provide the most appropriate response for your situation.

In Case of Emergency

If you are in crisis:

  • Call your local emergency number (911 in the US)
  • Contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 or 1-800-273-8255
  • Text the Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Go to your nearest emergency room

Privacy Notice

Your conversations with MindfulMate may be stored to improve the service. We recommend not sharing highly personal identifying information.

Professional Help

We strongly encourage seeking help from licensed mental health professionals for ongoing or serious concerns. MindfulMate should be viewed as a supplementary tool rather than a replacement for professional care.
