Mental Health Resources

Below is a comprehensive list of mental health resources that may be helpful. Remember that seeking professional help is important for mental health concerns.

Crisis Resources

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress

Phone: 988 or 1-800-273-8255

Website: suicidepreventionlifeline.org

Crisis Text Line

24/7 text-based crisis support

Text: HOME to 741741

Website: crisistextline.org

Emergency Services

For immediate danger to yourself or others

Call: 911 (US) or your local emergency number
